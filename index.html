Earth Day



When Is Earth Day Celebrated ?

The Earth Day, also known as the International Mother Earth Day, was first celebrated on April 22, 1970. The World Earth Day, also known as the International Mother Earth Day, is celebrated every year on April 22.

Why Is Earth Day Celebrated ?

That's why each year on April 22, more than a billion people celebrate Earth Day to protect the planet from things like pollution and deforestation. By taking part in activities like picking up litter and planting trees, we're making our world a happier, healthier place to live.

What Is The Theme Of World Earth Day 2022

This year marks the 52nd Earth Day, and the theme assigned by the Earth Day Organisation is 'Invest In Our Planet.' It aims at improving the climate, as well as the planet's health in every way possible.
